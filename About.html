Commitment to Safety and Excellence.

Policies

We provide Rates and Policies pages to assist in making sure your pets have everything they need in preparation for their stay with us.

Extending your stay

Any pets staying beyond their pre-scheduled checkout date without notifying us in advance may be subject to a $55.00 surcharge per day.

Holiday Fees

There is an additional $10.00 fee (per pet) for anyone checking out on holidays with special 3:00 pm -5:00 pm hours.

Feedings

We feed at approximately 6:00 am and 6:00 pm. Please feed your pet at home if you check-in after 6:00 pm. We serve Natural Balance dry food. If you bring your pet’s food from home, please package dry kibble in individual servings (such as in Baggies), or bring your measuring cup from home so we do not over or under feed.

Personal Possessions

Please label each of your pet’s belongings. You are welcome to bring two toys, treats and your pet’s favorite bedding to make their stay more enjoyable. However, we cannot be responsible for the condition of these possessions during your pet’s vacation. Soiled, wet or dirty bedding will be removed, bagged and our bedding will be provided at no charge.

Checking - in

Check-in is between 7:00 am and 4:00 pm Monday-Friday and 9:00 am – 4:00 pm Saturday. Sunday by appointment only. First time clients please try to check-in earlier in the day, offering your pet an opportunity to settle in before the daytime staff leaves for the night.

Checking - out

We like to have all dogs checked-out by 12:00 noon. Anyone checking-out after 12:00 noon will be charged a $35 Daycare fee. This does not apply to anyone checking out on holidays when we are only open between the hours of 3:00 pm and 5:00 pm.

Vaccinations

FOR DOGS: We require Rabies, Distemper/Parvo. Contact our office for more details.

Flea & Tick Prevention

If your pet is not on a preventative, we highly recommend they are covered to prevent them from getting any fleas or ticks

Deposits

Reservations for all new clients will be confirmed with a $50 non-refundable deposit. ALL holiday reservations will be confirmed with a $50 non-refundable deposit. Deposits are forfeited upon cancellation of reservation.

Form Of Payment

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover or cash. Checks are not accepted.

Live Mascam

In case you want to check in on your pet to see what he's doing, who she's playing with, or simply how they're settling into paradise, we have live webcams to catch all the highlights of your dog's stay. Just don't be surprised if you find yourself turning into a Maspet binge watcher!

Play Area cameras are viewable from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. every day. Suite Cameras are viewable 24 hours a day; If you are unable to locate your dog on the webcams, please chat our front desk and they can further direct you.

About Mascam

Only our customers that are boarding with us presently have access due to closed circuit privacy. To obtain webcam access, please call or stop by.

Webcams are only viewable in private suites, group rooms and outside. We do not have webcams in grooming or in kennel rooms due to the number of cameras it would take to cover those areas. If you have chosen private kennel you will only be able to see your dog outside not inside.

Please note: the web cameras are live streaming for your viewing pleasure. These cameras do not record and do not have the capability to play back camera feeds. Recording footage on another device, public representation or reproduction, and posting recordings or images on internet websites and social media channels is illegal and strictly prohibited.
